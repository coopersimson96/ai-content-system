# Removal Request Templates

Fill every `{placeholder}` from the user's Step 0 details before output. Never leave a blank for
the user. Keep them firm, professional, copy-paste ready.

---

## CCPA opt-out + deletion (US)

```
Subject: CCPA Request to Delete and Opt Out of Sale of Personal Information

To {broker name} Privacy Team,

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA), I am exercising my right to know, my
right to delete, and my right to opt out of the sale and sharing of my personal information.

I request that you:
1. Delete all personal information you hold about me (Cal. Civ. Code 1798.105).
2. Opt me out of any sale or sharing of my personal information (Cal. Civ. Code 1798.120).
3. Suppress my information so it does not reappear in future listings.

My identifying details for this request:
- Full name: {full name}
- Email: {email}
- Phone: {phone}
- City/State: {city}, {state}
- Listing URL (if applicable): {listing_url}

Please confirm completion in writing within 30 days as required by law. Do not sell, share, or
re-add my data going forward.

Regards,
{full name}
```

## GDPR erasure (EU/UK)

```
Subject: GDPR Article 17 Request, Erasure of Personal Data

To {broker name} Data Protection Officer,

Under Article 17 of the GDPR (right to erasure) and Article 21 (right to object to processing),
I request that you erase all personal data you hold about me and cease any processing or sale of it.

My details:
- Full name: {full name}
- Email: {email}
- Phone: {phone}
- Location: {city}, {country}
- Listing URL (if applicable): {listing_url}

Please confirm erasure in writing within 30 days (Article 12(3)). If you do not comply, I reserve
the right to lodge a complaint with my supervisory authority.

Regards,
{full name}
```

## Account deletion (dead accounts)

```
Subject: Permanent Account Deletion Request

To {company} Support,

Please permanently delete my account and all associated personal data under {CCPA / GDPR Article 17}.

- Account email: {email}
- Username (if known): {username}

I request written confirmation once deletion is complete, within 30 days. Please do not retain my
data beyond any narrow legal requirement.

Regards,
{full name}
```

## Suppression content angle prompt (for un-deletable results)

When generating 5 content angles to bury a result, each angle =
`{title}` + `{1-line summary}` + `{best platform}`, all targeting the same keywords as the bad
result (usually `{full name} {profession}`), so fresh pages out-rank it over 2-3 months.
