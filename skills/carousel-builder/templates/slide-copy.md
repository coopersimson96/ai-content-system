## Copy rules

- Short headlines beat clever headlines. < 12 words.
- The headline carries the slide. Support copy is optional.
- One thought per slide. If you can split a headline, split the slide.
- Bottom notes use small caps + `·` separators or "→" arrows. Keep under 8 words.
- Final-slide CTA must name **one specific keyword** and **one specific reward**.
